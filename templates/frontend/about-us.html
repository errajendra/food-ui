About Us

Welcome to Amritsari Tadke Mein!

Our Story

Amritsari Tadke Mein started with a passion for bringing authentic and delicious Punjabi flavors to Bangalore. Our journey began in the heart of BTM Layout - 2nd stage, where we set up our first outlet with a commitment to serving homemade-style meals prepared with love and care.

Our Mission

At Amritsari Tadke Mein, our mission is simple: to delight our customers with flavorful and wholesome meals that remind them of home-cooked goodness. We strive to source the freshest ingredients, handpick spices, and prepare each dish with meticulous attention to detail to ensure the highest quality and authenticity in every bite.

Our Philosophy

We believe in the power of good food to bring people together and create memorable experiences. Whether you're craving comforting classics or adventurous flavors, our diverse menu caters to all tastes and preferences. With a focus on taste, quality, and affordability, we aim to exceed our customers' expectations with every meal.

Why Choose Us?

  1. Authentic Punjabi flavors: Experience the rich and vibrant taste of Punjab with our traditional recipes passed down through generations.
  2. Fresh and wholesome ingredients: We source the finest ingredients to ensure the freshest and most flavorful meals for our customers.
  3. Convenient delivery: Enjoy the convenience of having delicious meals delivered straight to your doorstep, making mealtime hassle-free and enjoyable.
  4. Exceptional customer service: Our team is dedicated to providing excellent service and ensuring a pleasant dining experience for every customer.

Get in Touch

We'd love to hear from you! Whether you have feedback, questions, or simply want to say hello, feel free to reach out to us. You can contact us via WhatsApp at 8168572936 or email us at contact@atmkaro.in. Connect with us on social media to stay updated on our latest offerings and promotions.

Thank you for choosing Amritsari Tadke Mein. We look forward to serving you delicious meals that warm your heart and satisfy your cravings!
