Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Amritsari Tadke Mein ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects the personal information of our customers ("you" or "your") in connection with our Tiffin Service operations in Bangalore.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal information from customers:

  1. Name: We collect your name to personalize your Tiffin Service experience.
  2. Phone Number: We collect your phone number to facilitate communication regarding your orders and delivery.
  3. Location: We collect your location to ensure accurate delivery of your meals.

How We Collect Information

We collect personal information through the following methods:

  1. Website and Application: Information is collected when you use our website or mobile application to place orders or update your account details.
  2. WhatsApp: With your consent, we may collect information via WhatsApp for order processing and communication purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  1. Order Processing: To process and fulfill your Tiffin Service orders.
  2. Delivery Services: We share your location with third-party delivery services to ensure timely delivery of your meals.
  3. Customer Support: To provide customer support and assistance regarding your orders.
  4. Service Improvement: To analyze usage trends and improve our Tiffin Service offerings.

Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information, including phone numbers, with delivery personnel. However, we share location data with third-party delivery services solely for the purpose of delivering your meals.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Once your meal is delivered, we promptly delete location data from delivery personnel software.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You can do so by logging into our mobile application or website and accessing your account settings.

Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

Compliance

We comply with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or data practices, please contact us at contact@atmkaro.in.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the revised policy on our website.
